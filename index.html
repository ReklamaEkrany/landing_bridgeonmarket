Hydrogen Water System — innovative generation and sales platform for hydrogen water

A device for producing, storing, and dispensing hydrogen‑enriched drinking water for home and commercial use. Core idea: saturating water with molecular hydrogen (H₂) while maintaining a stable concentration during bottling and storage.

Deep‑Tech Core

  • • Low‑temperature PEM electrolyzer with a next‑gen membrane (in‑house design).
  • • Unique aluminium sealed chamber 13×6 cm with a built‑in display for live parameters.
  • • High‑efficiency ionization system with optimized current usage (24 V, 10 A stabilized).
  • • Integrated self‑diagnostics and ESP32 control with Bluetooth/Wi‑Fi.
  • • Achieves 1.2–1.6 ppm H₂ — a clinically meaningful antioxidant range.
